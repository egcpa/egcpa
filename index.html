Medication Distribution


Missing a single dose of their medications may seem harmless at first glance, but this action, especially when done repetitively, can lead to other health risks and possible worsening of your senior’s current health conditions. Don’t allow them to suffer from harmful symptoms due to not taking their medications as prescribed. Our caregivers can help manage their medication intake through constant reminders and proper organization of their drugs for easier access and convenience.​

Feeding & Meal Preps


Being mindful of your senior's nutrient intake can be complicated. Not only will you have to consider their dietary requirements, but you also have to think of factors, like possible allergies, food storage, and food-medication interactions. Our caregivers can assist you with this task. Our meal planning program and feeding assistance ensures that your loved one eats a balanced diet that won't affect the medication plan they are currently on.

Toileting & Incontinence
Care

We uphold your dignity when assisting you with your bowel and bladder routines.

Some seniors may find that emptying their bladders or controlling their bowel movements becomes difficult as they age. While this isn't an unfamiliar situation for  ​caregivers, your loved one may feel uncomfortable to ask for help in situations like this. We guarantee you that your loved one will receive adequate assistance to the bathroom or in case of an accident while respecting their preferences and boundaries to ensure they remain comfortable in our care. We will also work with your senior's physician in rare cases when incontinence gets worse or certain symptoms develop.
