Errands & Shopping

We accompany you to important errands, appointments, and trips so you feel protected at all times.​When your senior travels alone, they meet so many risks, whether during commuting or accidents at their appointment venues themselves. Availing of our errands and shopping services can reduce that risk. Depending on your senior’s mobility and stamina, we can offer to do the errands for them so they can relax comfortably at home or be a trusted companion they can rely on during their travels. Our caregivers can help your loved one with:​
Grocery shopping
Medical appointments, like check-ups and hospital visits
Acquiring transportation
Accompaniment to social and community-centered activities
Visits to friends and relatives
Picking up prescriptions
Planning and scheduling appointments
Sending or picking up mail
